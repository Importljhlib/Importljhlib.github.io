Short bio

I am an undergraduate student at Soongsil University, double majoring in Global Media and AI Convergence and an undergraduate researcher at the Reality Lab advised by Prof. Heewon Kim. I have a strong interest in developing novel algorithms and AI technologies applicable to optics, AR displays, and holography. As an undergraduate researcher, my current focus is on creating AI systems capable of generating holograms in real time for use in AR glasses.
